Gang Tokenomics

$GANG Token

$GANG - is the native token and the core currency of Sui Gang.
$GANG tokens can be obtained in 3 ways:
  • By holding Sui Gang NFT.
  • By participating in project activities.
  • By stacking NFT.

$GANG Distribution

The token has a fixed total supply of 100 000 000 $GANG tokens, provisionally allocated to the following areas:
  • Team - 6%
    Team tokens will be unlocked one year after the launch.
  • Community rewards - 20%
    These tokens are divided into two categories:
    • Airdrop for NFT Collection Sui Gang holders - 13%
    • Airdrop for project community - 7%
  • Farm rewards - 70%
    The token emission is calculated for 2 years, with 4.16% of the tokens (2 912 000 $GANG) from the pool allocated each month. Payments for staking will be credited to your account daily. The amount of tokens you can earn depends on:
    • Quantity of NFTs that are staked.
    • Rarity of NFTs that are staked.
  • Sui Gang reserve - 4%
    Reserved for the project needs: liquidity, marketing, etc.
