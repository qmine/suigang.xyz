Gang F.A.Q.

  • What is SUI?
    Sui is a decentralized, proof of stake blockchain with horizontally scalable throughput and storage. Learn more about SUI on their website.
  • What SUI wallet should I use?
    We recommend using either Sui Wallet or Suiet Wallet.
  • What is DAO?
    The Dao mechanism will initially be organized in Discord, where NFT holders will be able to influence the development of the project. After $GANG token is released, holders will be able to vote on our website.
  • How can I receive $GANG tokens?
    The initial distribution of $GANG tokens will be through the airdrop for NFT holders, after the launch of staking. It will be possible to stake your NFT and receive tokens as well.
  • How will the royalty be distributed?
    On 25th of every month, a snapshot of holders will be taken. SUI coins will be sent out on the first of the following month. Reward depends on how many NFTs you had on your wallet at the time of the snapshot.

    Distribution example:
    Lets assume total royalty by the end of January is 2222 $SUI tokens and during the snapshot on January 25th there were 10 Sui Gang NFTs on your wallet. In this case, you will be rewarded with 10 $SUI tokens on February 1st.
  • Total supply NFTs and price?
    2222 NFTs. Price has not been set yet.
